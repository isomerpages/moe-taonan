<p>We desire our girls to achieve their fullest potential through positive
character-based teachings and activities. We aim to do so by equipping,
empowering and enabling every girl to be a leader.</p>
<p>The programme is centred on three key areas:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Leadership development</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Character development</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Service to the community</p>
</li>
</ol>
